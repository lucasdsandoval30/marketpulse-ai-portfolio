Product

Built to explain why a market story matters

Ranked article intelligence

Scores news with recency, source quality, market exposure, novelty, sentiment intensity, and connected narrative importance.

Narrative clustering

Groups related articles into developing market stories using local storage, embeddings, cluster scoring, and evidence-aware summaries.

Ticker exposure mapping

Separates direct tickers, second-order tickers, and sector ETFs so the user can inspect likely market read-throughs.

Noise reduction

Collapses syndicated duplicate headlines so repeated domains do not inflate narrative volume, source diversity, or ranking confidence.

Engineering

A practical system for messy financial news

01

Ingestion and normalization

Source adapters collect articles, extract tickers/entities, normalize metadata, and store evidence in SQLite.

02

Ranking and clustering

Local rankers score impact while the narrative engine clusters related stories and filters duplicate evidence.

03

LLM summaries with guardrails

Structured prompts produce JSON summaries, ticker exposure reasoning, limitations, and narrative context.
