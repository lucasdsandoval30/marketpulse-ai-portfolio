Product

Built to practice working with real market news data

Ranked article intelligence

Scores articles using recency, source quality, market exposure, novelty, sentiment, and how closely they connect to larger market stories.

Narrative clustering

Groups related articles into market stories using local storage, clustering logic, scoring, and summaries.

Ticker exposure mapping

Separates direct tickers, possible secondary tickers, and sector ETFs so a user can inspect how a story might connect to different parts of the market.

Noise reduction

Reduces duplicate headlines so repeated syndicated articles do not make a story look more important than it is.

Engineering

A practical system for learning with messy financial news

01

Ingestion and normalization

Source adapters collect articles, extract tickers and entities, normalize metadata, and store evidence in SQLite.

02

Ranking and clustering

Local rankers score article impact while the narrative logic groups related stories and filters duplicate evidence.

03

LLM summaries with guardrails

Structured prompts test whether LLMs can summarize articles, explain ticker exposure, and list limitations clearly.
